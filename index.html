Andrew Tower

5850 San Felipe, Ste 500 · Houston, TX 77057 · (713) 364-4701 · andy@atowerassoc.com

Andrew P. Tower has over twenty-five years of experience as an attorney and counselor at law, primarily working on complex business and intellectual property, transactional and litigation matters in the energy industry.  He was a partner at Howrey LLP, formerly one of the largest intellectual property firms in the world.

Mr. Tower represents clients over a wide range of energy industry-related commercial and intellectual property matters, including freedom to operate searches, asset management and licensing.

Mr. Tower has represented clients in industries involving a wide range of technologies, including oilfield equipment and oil country tubular goods, petrochemical processing equipment, industrial valves, telecommunications, geological software, computer systems and software, power filtration, consumer electronics, optical disk drives, transportation and textiles. He also represents clients in arbitrations and in proceedings before the United States Patent and Trademark Office.

Mr. Tower has successfully handled numerous complex commercial disputes, obtaining multi-million dollar awards through trials, settlements and arbitration, as well as defense against multi-million dollar claims, through jury verdict, summary judgments and indemnified settlements.

Prior to practicing law, Mr. Tower worked as a mechanical and petroleum engineer for a division of Baker Hughes.
